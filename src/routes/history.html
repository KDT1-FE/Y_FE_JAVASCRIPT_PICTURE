  • AZKABAN

  • Location : Somewhere in the North Sea, United Kingdom.

  • Affiliation : Magical Law Enforcement.

  • Warden : Prison Director, Dementors, Assigned Aurors.

  • History :
    The tower was originally owned by the dark wizard Ekrizdys. He casually killed sailors passing by for fun, and his henchman Dementors also sucked away people's happiness, infusing them with depression and fear. After his death, the Disguise Charm was lifted, and the magical authorities discovered it, deciding to repurpose it as a prison.

  • In addition, there is another prison called Nurmengard located somewhere in Austria.
